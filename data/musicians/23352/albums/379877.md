## 简介

<div>
Angus Drive is an EP released in 2002 by Canadian singer Avril Lavigne featuring songs all previously released and featured on her 2002 studio album, Let Go.  The Angus Drive EP is a very rare CD. It is a promotional only release. It was released in advance to DJ's and radio stations and a few select people as part of a small promotional campaign. It is rumored that only 1000 or even less than 1,000 of these were released.
