## 简介

<div>
艾薇儿即将发行的第五张个人专辑的第二波主打。<br><br>
<br><br>
"Rock N Roll" is a song by Franco-Canadian recording artist Avril Lavigne. It was released as the second single from her upcoming self-titled fifth studio album by Epic Records on 23 August 2013. The song was written by Lavigne, David Hodges, Chad Kroeger, Jacob Kasher Hindlin, Rickard B. Göransson and Peter Svensson and was produced by Peter Svensson and Martin Johnson.
