Here are the mind of the Buddha and Budhsatava which is completely non-conceptual wisdom's significance mantras: The mantras containing names of Buddha and Bodhisattva are beautified with Amaho voice accompanied by modern instruments, drum, flute and guitar, is produced in CD for everyone.<br><br>
