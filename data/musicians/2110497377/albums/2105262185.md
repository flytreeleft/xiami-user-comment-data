## 简介

<div>
「花火」关于一个梦，一个有你的梦。<br><br>
梦里我们没有说话，有一个透明的屋顶，我们坐在花草的中间，看着夜空寂静而美好。<br><br>
花火在一瞬间把整个天空点亮。<br><br>
因为这个梦，我开始相信短暂和永恒的长度相差无几。<br><br>
最绚烂的那一刻永远燃烧着。<br><br>
<br><br>
「Hana-bi」is about a dream, a dream with you.<br><br>
In that invisible dreamland, we are surrounded by summer trees and flowers, transparent roof overhead, every moment with you are tender and fragrant at night, the flash I hide it deep in my memory, when Hana-bi blooming, lighting the planet…<br><br>
After the dream, I choose to believe, those brief and pretty moment are not that far away from so-called eternity. <br><br>
All of your love are in flame, stay in the most gorgeous moment.
</div>
