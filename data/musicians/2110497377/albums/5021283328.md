## 简介

<div>
“借你的眼，看这世界一天。”<br><br>
2020盛夏，桃子假象Peach&nbsp;Illusion乐队带来全新概念EP——「借你的眼」（Let&nbsp;Me&nbsp;Enter&nbsp;Your&nbsp;Eyes）<br><br>
整张专辑与一个故事相连，故事以漫画形式呈现。<br><br>
专辑A-side与B-side的四首歌名与漫画的四章节对应。<br><br>
“人类丢失的美好是天上的星星，当你发现这些美好的时候，星星才会回到地球，留在你身边。”<br><br>
故事讲述了在时间和海浪中穿行的我们来到“桃子星球”，唤醒了“桃吉”这只桃怪，借着“桃吉”的眼睛，我们看到“桃子星球”的两面——那些将我们缠绕的「生活的茧」，那些被我们遗忘的「生活碎片」…而这一切都是人类世界的映射。<br><br>
由主唱圈圈撰写脚本、漫画家头爷爷touyeye主笔的「桃子星球」系列漫画vol.1与专辑同步上线。<br><br>
故事里的那些碎片，散落在歌的字里行间，愿你能拾掇一片。<br><br>
————————————————<br><br>
Track&nbsp;List<br><br>
A-side&nbsp;of&nbsp;peach&nbsp;planet<br><br>
01-「Mirror」<br><br>
故事的开始，通往“桃子星球”的一面镜子向我们打开。镜子里我们以为看到的是对方，原来看到的是自己。<br><br>
主唱圈圈与吉他手C.c的首次合唱，两个人的对话也是和自己的对话。<br><br>
娜影动态的鼓组和学家内敛的贝斯，让音乐在时间和海浪中长出想象外的花朵。<br><br>
宇宙中无法被理解、被定义的，我们不断遗忘、丢失和寻找的，那些之于我们最重要的，都随着一面镜子的破碎，消失在风中。<br><br>
02-「借你的眼」<br><br>
同名主打《借你的眼》，解开／揭开桃子世界的不同答案，一面在追逐，一面在等待。<br><br>
风跟在背后，流线形的吉他和律动推着故事往前走。<br><br>
随着"Let&nbsp;me&nbsp;enter&nbsp;your&nbsp;eyes"&nbsp;机器人式的问话，我们睁开眼，看到褪去平淡生活的茧，里面藏着的碎片——没有说出口的话，多么难懂的美，所有的事与愿违和把你放在好梦的结尾。<br><br>
你渴望借谁的眼，看到怎样的世界呢？<br><br>
<br><br>
B-side&nbsp;of&nbsp;peach&nbsp;planet<br><br>
03-「无星的夜」<br><br>
简短的四句歌词，Lullaby一般的自言自语，以桃子钟爱的印象派古典乐语言表达。<br><br>
钢琴与吟唱的华尔兹轻巧地划过湖面，一艘纸船载着所有的星星坠入五拍的急流，梦结束前的那一刻，我们躺在月光铺满的大海上。<br><br>
04-「透明色」<br><br>
圈圈写于2016年的demo，四年后桃子的重编。<br><br>
保留了少女眼里清澈的对爱的想象，又使画面更斑斓、目光更坚定、情感更有力量。<br><br>
透明是海浪，温柔而强大。河流是爱的比喻，我们在水中改变了彼此的形状。<br><br>
跨越海洋后，河流是否还是河流？爱过我以后，你是否还是你？<br><br>
<br><br>
bonus&nbsp;track<br><br>
05-「I&nbsp;Love&nbsp;You&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;way&nbsp;You&nbsp;Love&nbsp;Me（粤）」<br><br>
继「陌生来电」后，桃子粤语情歌第二辑，由乐队中的两位桃子女孩圈圈（词曲创作）&amp;娜影（编曲制作）共同打造，审视爱的多重形态与亲密关系中的模仿行为。<br><br>
也许爱就是一面这样的镜子——我们站在镜子的两端——<br><br>
“I&nbsp;Love&nbsp;You&nbsp;||&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;way&nbsp;||&nbsp;You&nbsp;Love&nbsp;Me”<br><br>
我们总是能从另一个人身上学会去爱，学会想念，学会离开，也学会无声的伤害。<br><br>
这注定是颗不太一样的桃子，穿上电子乐的外衣，带着些许冷漠、黑暗的色彩，伴随着无间歇的浪潮、碎拍，两位女孩刻画着桃子背后迷离的真相。<br><br>

“What&nbsp;you&nbsp;see&nbsp;from&nbsp;window&nbsp;to&nbsp;the&nbsp;Universe&nbsp;of&nbsp;life?&nbsp;Let&nbsp;me&nbsp;enter&nbsp;your&nbsp;eyes…”<br><br>
In&nbsp;the&nbsp;midsummer&nbsp;of&nbsp;2020,&nbsp;Peach&nbsp;Illusion&nbsp;release&nbsp;a&nbsp;Concept&nbsp;EP&nbsp;“Let&nbsp;Me&nbsp;Enter&nbsp;Your&nbsp;Eyes”.<br><br>
The&nbsp;entire&nbsp;album&nbsp;is&nbsp;linked&nbsp;to&nbsp;a&nbsp;story,&nbsp;which&nbsp;is&nbsp;presented&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;form&nbsp;of&nbsp;a&nbsp;comic&nbsp;book&nbsp;“Peach&nbsp;Planet&nbsp;vol.1”&nbsp;(Script&nbsp;by&nbsp;vocal&nbsp;Quanquan,&nbsp;Illustrate&nbsp;by&nbsp;Touyeye).&nbsp;The&nbsp;four&nbsp;song&nbsp;titles&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;album&nbsp;A-side&nbsp;and&nbsp;B-side&nbsp;correspond&nbsp;to&nbsp;the&nbsp;four&nbsp;chapters&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;comic&nbsp;book.<br><br>
"When&nbsp;you&nbsp;turn&nbsp;a&nbsp;blind&nbsp;eye&nbsp;to&nbsp;those&nbsp;broken&nbsp;pieces&nbsp;of&nbsp;your&nbsp;life&nbsp;story,&nbsp;it&nbsp;would&nbsp;escape&nbsp;to&nbsp;the&nbsp;heaven,&nbsp;to&nbsp;the&nbsp;moon,&nbsp;being&nbsp;a&nbsp;star.&nbsp;Only&nbsp;if&nbsp;you&nbsp;find&nbsp;it,&nbsp;it&nbsp;comes&nbsp;back&nbsp;to&nbsp;the&nbsp;earth,&nbsp;don’t&nbsp;leave&nbsp;you&nbsp;again.”<br><br>
In&nbsp;the&nbsp;story,&nbsp;the&nbsp;peach&nbsp;girl&nbsp;and&nbsp;the&nbsp;peach&nbsp;boy&nbsp;had&nbsp;a&nbsp;time&nbsp;travel&nbsp;through&nbsp;a&nbsp;Mirror,&nbsp;counter-clockwise&nbsp;time&nbsp;and&nbsp;huge&nbsp;waves&nbsp;brought&nbsp;them&nbsp;to&nbsp;Peach&nbsp;Planet.&nbsp;They&nbsp;didn’t&nbsp;know&nbsp;there&nbsp;are&nbsp;peach&nbsp;elves&nbsp;living&nbsp;and&nbsp;sleeping&nbsp;inside&nbsp;the&nbsp;cocoon&nbsp;on&nbsp;trees&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;planet,&nbsp;until&nbsp;one&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;elves&nbsp;named&nbsp;Peach&nbsp;Ji&nbsp;waken&nbsp;up&nbsp;by&nbsp;them.&nbsp;Entering&nbsp;Ji’s&nbsp;eyes&nbsp;by&nbsp;its&nbsp;superpower,&nbsp;they&nbsp;see&nbsp;two&nbsp;sides&nbsp;of&nbsp;Peach&nbsp;Planet:&nbsp;all&nbsp;those&nbsp;cocoon&nbsp;of&nbsp;life&nbsp;common&nbsp;people&nbsp;bound,&nbsp;all&nbsp;those&nbsp;sparkling&nbsp;moment&nbsp;people&nbsp;chose&nbsp;to&nbsp;forget&nbsp;…Everything&nbsp;is&nbsp;the&nbsp;reflection&nbsp;of&nbsp;human&nbsp;world.<br><br>
Hint&nbsp;and&nbsp;cipher&nbsp;hidden&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;songs,&nbsp;wish&nbsp;you&nbsp;will&nbsp;see.&nbsp;<br><br>
————————————————<br><br>
Track&nbsp;List<br><br>
·&nbsp;A-side&nbsp;of&nbsp;peach&nbsp;planet<br><br>
01-Mirror<br><br>
At&nbsp;the&nbsp;beginning&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;story,&nbsp;we&nbsp;flew&nbsp;through&nbsp;a&nbsp;Mirror&nbsp;to&nbsp;Peach&nbsp;Planet.&nbsp;In&nbsp;the&nbsp;mirror,&nbsp;somehow&nbsp;we&nbsp;exchanged&nbsp;our&nbsp;faces&nbsp;to&nbsp;be&nbsp;one&nbsp;another,&nbsp;even&nbsp;though&nbsp;we&nbsp;didn't&nbsp;know&nbsp;those&nbsp;were&nbsp;our&nbsp;own&nbsp;faces.<br><br>
It&nbsp;is&nbsp;the&nbsp;first&nbsp;time&nbsp;two&nbsp;lead&nbsp;vocals&nbsp;appears&nbsp;in&nbsp;Peach&nbsp;Illusion's&nbsp;song&nbsp;(Quanquan&nbsp;and&nbsp;C.c),&nbsp;the&nbsp;peach&nbsp;girl&nbsp;and&nbsp;the&nbsp;peach&nbsp;boy&nbsp;dialogue&nbsp;with&nbsp;each&nbsp;other&nbsp;in&nbsp;music,&nbsp;and&nbsp;also&nbsp;dialogue&nbsp;with&nbsp;themselves.<br><br>
The&nbsp;dynamic&nbsp;drums&nbsp;played&nbsp;by&nbsp;Nayoung&nbsp;create&nbsp;an&nbsp;extension&nbsp;of&nbsp;space,&nbsp;and&nbsp;the&nbsp;connotative&nbsp;bass&nbsp;line&nbsp;presented&nbsp;by&nbsp;Xuejia&nbsp;become&nbsp;a&nbsp;soft&nbsp;and&nbsp;fertile&nbsp;land,&nbsp;a&nbsp;fabulous&nbsp;flower&nbsp;bloom&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;land&nbsp;of&nbsp;Peach&nbsp;Planet,&nbsp;covered&nbsp;up&nbsp;with&nbsp;time&nbsp;and&nbsp;tides.<br><br>
What&nbsp;we&nbsp;can't&nbsp;see&nbsp;and&nbsp;reveal&nbsp;in&nbsp;this&nbsp;infinite&nbsp;Universe,&nbsp;what&nbsp;we&nbsp;lost&nbsp;and&nbsp;found,&nbsp;and&nbsp;what&nbsp;we&nbsp;cherish&nbsp;the&nbsp;most,&nbsp;all&nbsp;disappear&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;wind&nbsp;with&nbsp;the&nbsp;shattering&nbsp;of&nbsp;a&nbsp;Mirror.<br><br>
<br><br>
02-Let&nbsp;Me&nbsp;Enter&nbsp;Your&nbsp;Eyes<br><br>
In&nbsp;the&nbsp;title&nbsp;track&nbsp;Let&nbsp;Me&nbsp;Enter&nbsp;Your&nbsp;Eyes,&nbsp;we&nbsp;see&nbsp;the&nbsp;peach&nbsp;world&nbsp;with&nbsp;different&nbsp;eyes.&nbsp;When&nbsp;we&nbsp;try&nbsp;to&nbsp;find&nbsp;the&nbsp;answer,&nbsp;we&nbsp;know&nbsp;there&nbsp;is&nbsp;a&nbsp;key&nbsp;lying&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;box&nbsp;of&nbsp;life.&nbsp;When&nbsp;we&nbsp;wait&nbsp;for&nbsp;an&nbsp;answer,&nbsp;the&nbsp;unlocked&nbsp;box&nbsp;lying&nbsp;quietly&nbsp;in&nbsp;our&nbsp;dream.<br><br>
Following&nbsp;the&nbsp;pace&nbsp;of&nbsp;wind,&nbsp;the&nbsp;flowing&nbsp;guitar&nbsp;riff&nbsp;and&nbsp;groove&nbsp;roll&nbsp;up&nbsp;the&nbsp;story&nbsp;and&nbsp;move&nbsp;on.<br><br>
Bot-monologue&nbsp;"Let&nbsp;me&nbsp;enter&nbsp;your&nbsp;eyes"&nbsp;is&nbsp;placed&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;middle&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;song,&nbsp;it&nbsp;opens&nbsp;our&nbsp;eyes&nbsp;to&nbsp;another&nbsp;side&nbsp;of&nbsp;our&nbsp;life:&nbsp;we&nbsp;break&nbsp;the&nbsp;cocoon,&nbsp;secrets&nbsp;and&nbsp;whispers&nbsp;hidden&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;corner&nbsp;are&nbsp;lit&nbsp;up&nbsp;by&nbsp;stars,&nbsp;if&nbsp;we&nbsp;can&nbsp;make&nbsp;it&nbsp;as&nbsp;what&nbsp;we&nbsp;want,&nbsp;will&nbsp;you&nbsp;let&nbsp;me&nbsp;enter&nbsp;your&nbsp;eyes,&nbsp;standing&nbsp;together&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;end&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;story?<br><br>
<br><br>
·&nbsp;B-side&nbsp;of&nbsp;peach&nbsp;planet<br><br>
03-Starless&nbsp;Night<br><br>
The&nbsp;lyrics&nbsp;consist&nbsp;of&nbsp;only&nbsp;four&nbsp;sentences.&nbsp;<br><br>
C.c&nbsp;plays&nbsp;the&nbsp;piano&nbsp;at&nbsp;night,&nbsp;Quanquan&nbsp;sings&nbsp;this&nbsp;song&nbsp;as&nbsp;a&nbsp;lullaby,&nbsp;they&nbsp;describe&nbsp;the&nbsp;image&nbsp;of&nbsp;Starless&nbsp;Night&nbsp;in&nbsp;language&nbsp;of&nbsp;Peach's&nbsp;favorite&nbsp;impressionist&nbsp;classical&nbsp;music.<br><br>

<br><br>
04-Transparent<br><br>
Quanquan&nbsp;wrote&nbsp;Transparent&nbsp;and&nbsp;made&nbsp;a&nbsp;demo&nbsp;in&nbsp;2016,&nbsp;Peach&nbsp;Illusion&nbsp;re-arranged&nbsp;and&nbsp;produced&nbsp;it&nbsp;four&nbsp;years&nbsp;later.&nbsp;The&nbsp;imagination&nbsp;of&nbsp;love&nbsp;from&nbsp;Quanquan's&nbsp;girlish&nbsp;eyes&nbsp;is&nbsp;combined&nbsp;with&nbsp;the&nbsp;picture&nbsp;drawn&nbsp;by&nbsp;Peach&nbsp;Illusion,&nbsp;the&nbsp;colors,&nbsp;voice&nbsp;and&nbsp;emotion&nbsp;gets&nbsp;brighter&nbsp;and&nbsp;more&nbsp;powerful.<br><br>
River&nbsp;in&nbsp;this&nbsp;song&nbsp;is&nbsp;a&nbsp;metaphor&nbsp;for&nbsp;love,&nbsp;we&nbsp;change&nbsp;our&nbsp;shapes&nbsp;underwater,&nbsp;we&nbsp;hug&nbsp;each&nbsp;other&nbsp;like&nbsp;waves,&nbsp;which&nbsp;is&nbsp;transparent,&nbsp;tender&nbsp;and&nbsp;strong.<br><br>
At&nbsp;the&nbsp;end&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;song,&nbsp;the&nbsp;girl&nbsp;murmurs&nbsp;"Love&nbsp;me&nbsp;like&nbsp;a&nbsp;river,&nbsp;across&nbsp;the&nbsp;sea...",&nbsp;and&nbsp;the&nbsp;boy&nbsp;repeats&nbsp;"You&nbsp;love&nbsp;me,&nbsp;you&nbsp;love&nbsp;me..."<br><br>
<br><br>
·&nbsp;Bonus&nbsp;Track<br><br>
