## 简介

<div>
<p>夏夜晚风原曲是来自伍佰于1997年夏夜晚风专辑。在今年芝加哥寒冷的冬天，终于有机会看了蔡明亮导演的作品爱情万岁，看完后突然想起了夏夜晚风这首歌。</p><p></p><p>Summer Night Wind was written and sung by the legendary Taiwanese singer Wu Bai in his 1997 Album. I have always loved this song since I heard it. And earlier this year, I finally got the chance to watch Tsai Ming Liang's movie Vive L'Amour. This movie reminds me of this song so much, blue, slow and sorrowful and I feel touched by the lyrics, so I decided to cover it during the chicago winter before the february valentine's day.</p>
</div>
