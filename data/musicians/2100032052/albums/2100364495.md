## 简介

<div>
«Dominicano» is a third neoclassical album by composer and pianist Egor Grushin. This album consists of two parts. The first part is a kind of story, which has two important characters. In music these characters are embodied as piano and cello, which emotionally complement each other. This story has a beginning , development, culmination and ending. The main theme of the story is Dominican cathedral in Lviv, which inspired the composer to create this music story. The second part of the album shows us something new bright individual style of neoclassical composer. “Dominicano” was released on April 2014. <br><br>
<br><br>
Piano by Egor Grushin <br><br>

'Apart from inclusions in box office movie soundtracks, the elegant combination of piano and strings has been sadly pushed away from the mainstream. It is no longer seen as fashionable and no longer yearned for by the majority of today’s audiences, and though timeless, it’s slowly becoming more and more of a memory. It is a sad event, and one certainly unfitting the style, but in Egor Grushin classical music might just have found a new champion...' <br><br>
<br><br>
'...His mastery of music in that way is a wonder to behold, but arguably the greatest thing that Egor achieves in ‘DOMINICANO’ is to make the simple combination of piano and strings beautiful and fashionable once more, and it that’s not worth listening to, then what is?'
