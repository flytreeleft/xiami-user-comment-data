## 简介

<div>
《ABSOLUTION》 is a second full-length album by Egor Grushin. Whole album – is a diversity of views and conclusions about some moments of his life. The author has experienced a large number of impressions that influenced the emotional background of the album. All tracks differ in an emotional color: some compositions are dramatical and militant, other – calm and meditative. Here is an expression of the musician about his life experience which has been imprinted in to his album: <br><br>
«In «ABSOLUTION» I opened my soul and asked forgiveness about some things. When the album was complete, I felt absolutely calm. I got peace of mind which sought» <br><br>
