## 简介

<div>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color: rgb(255, 102, 0);"><strong>⊙五首Peggy全新创作 五种Peggy浪漫配方 用最新鲜的方式 说她最擅长的故事<br><br>
</strong></span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new=""><br><br>
浪漫的样貌，从来不止一种。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">在众人簇拥下放声大哭，叫做浪漫</span><span lang="EN-US">…<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">独自一人躲在棉被里头微笑，也叫做浪漫</span><span lang="EN-US">…<br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">当满载浪漫的马戏团浩浩荡荡翻山越岭而来，</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">这将会是一场非比寻常的绝妙体验</span><span lang="EN-US">…<br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">继</span><span lang="EN-US">2009</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">年连续推出『美好的』及『雪人』两张全创作概念专辑获得各界好评回响之后，这次</span><span lang="EN-US">Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">将率领『马戏团</span><span lang="EN-US">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">号』带给大家充满奇幻的异色浪漫！</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">『马戏团</span><span lang="EN-US">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">号』迷你专辑收录了</span><span lang="EN-US">Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">精心挑选的</span><span lang="EN-US">5</span><span style="font-family:<br>
宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">首词曲创作，以</span><span lang="EN-US">“</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">浪漫</span><span lang="EN-US">”</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">概念引领乐迷进入马戏团帐篷内的迷你世界。在这个精巧瑰丽的空间里，有着充满惊喜人生的「巧克力之吻」、无私奉献的「如果我</span><span lang="EN-US">…</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">」、默默守护的「游泳圈」、法国电影式的「樱桃色情人」和傻的绝对的「魔术师」。不论对资深乐迷或是相见恨晚的乐迷而言，『马戏团</span><span lang="EN-US">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">号』都是绝对不容错失收藏的珍品！</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="color: rgb(255, 102, 0);"><strong><span class="in1"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">⊙</span></span><span class="in1"><span style="font-family: 宋体;" times="" new="">梦幻团队马戏团精心配制『马戏团</span><span lang="EN-US">1</span></span><span class="in1"><span style="font-family: 宋体;" times="" new="">号』<br><br>
</span></span></strong></span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
2003</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">年由</span><span lang="EN-US">Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">率领一班年轻乐手组成的乐团</span><span lang="EN-US">“</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">马戏团</span><span lang="EN-US"> Le irque”</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">（团名取自</span><span lang="EN-US">Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">创作「爱上马戏团团长」，收录于『美好的』专辑！），随着</span><span lang="EN-US">Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">走过大小城市，感动过大小朋友。身怀绝技的团员，就好比隐身在马戏团帐篷里的高手，戏幕升起的一刻，总是能和她一起用音乐燃烧着热情，带给人无尽的欢笑与泪水。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
<br><br>
Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">和团员合作无间的默契，也在充满愉悦气氛的录音过程中表露无遗。对于身负专辑制作人重任，并首度以乐团形式来制作『马戏团</span><span lang="EN-US">1</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">号』的</span><span lang="EN-US">Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">她说：「其实并没有想要强调是一张乐团专辑，也没有要要变身成为乐团，只是觉得找到了梦幻团队，想尝试用一种乐团形式，直接碰撞的方式来编曲。或许我身体里没有流着太多乐团的血液，但是我绝对有着对乐团满满的热情！」</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
<br><br>
</span><strong><span style="color: rgb(255, 102, 0);"><span class="in1"><span style="font-family: 宋体;">⊙</span></span><span class="in1"><span style="font-family: 宋体;" times="" new="">曲目介绍<br><br>
</span></span></span></strong><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-bidi-font-family:宋体"><br><br>
★</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">巧克力之吻</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">浪漫像颗微苦又回甘的巧克力，含在嘴里就能尝到诱人的浓郁。只要和舌尖分享一个令人兴奋的秘密，一场脸红心跳的冒险就要来临</span><span lang="EN-US">…<br><br>
<br><br>
Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">和马戏团玩乐出轻松又刺激的轻融合爵士</span><span lang="EN-US"> (Fusion Jazz)</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:<br>
" times="" new="">，在</span><span lang="EN-US">Cocktail Drums</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:<br>
" times="" new="">的带领下，微醺的浪漫就此蔓延</span><span lang="EN-US">…<br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-bidi-font-family:宋体">★</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">如果我</span><span lang="EN-US">…<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">浪漫也可以是无私又诡谲的牺牲，如果我少了一只手、如果我已经不在</span><span lang="EN-US">… </span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">我仍然会一直看着你陪着你听着你，甚至祝福你</span><span lang="EN-US">…<br><br>
<br><br>
Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">在飞机上所写的三拍子小品，以电吉他和大提琴作为主要乐器，淡淡的唱着生命中最深刻的分离与祝福。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-bidi-font-family:宋体">★</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">游泳圈</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">浪漫像一只孤单的游泳圈，载浮载沈的伴随妳渡过晴天或者雨天，即使孤单随着浪花渲染了整个海面，游泳圈也将默默守护在妳身边</span><span lang="EN-US">…<br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">创作灵感来自</span><span lang="EN-US">Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">小时候读过的一本童书绘本，记忆中几幅小女孩独自在海边和自己玩耍的景象，拼凑成了由钢琴和大提琴声交织而成的孤单浪漫。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-bidi-font-family:宋体">★</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">樱桃色情人</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">浪漫是一个腼腆的樱桃色情人，就连最深沈的思念都藏在樱桃色的酒窝里，只有成为他的情人随他进入梦境，才能够品尝樱桃吻的滋味以及甜蜜。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">节奏蓝调中混合着轻柔的放克，搭配优雅的弦乐四重奏，富蕴淡淡的法式都会风情。</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-bidi-font-family:宋体">★</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">魔术师</span><span lang="EN-US"><br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">自负的魔术师误解了浪漫的定义，他的浪漫总是那么笨拙又那么绝对，当爱人在他的魔术下一点一点的消失了声音还有自由，他还是自顾自的变着他所误解的浪漫的魔术</span><span lang="EN-US">…<br><br>
<br><br>
</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">一场极短篇的魔幻电影，融合古典和电子风格，透过和谐又冲突的编曲和乐句，加上宛如诗歌式的和声，衬托出</span><span lang="EN-US">Peggy</span><span style="font-family:宋体;mso-ascii-font-family:" times="" new="">古灵精怪的音乐个性。</span></p>
</div>
