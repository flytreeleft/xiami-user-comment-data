## 简介

<div>
Kero One &amp; Azure are veteran Korean-American rapper/producer/Djs born and raised in the SF Bay Area, almost like twins but almost a decade difference in age.  Their new album is a duet project that showcases their love for witty rhymes, storytelling, and conceptual songs.  Even though Kero has been producing #1 hit songs for Kpop artists and Azure is from the San Francisco Bay Area’s most successful rap crew (Hbk Gang), they call this project their most underground hiphop project because they want to strip things down to just the foundation of hiphop with no interest in pop charts. <br><br>
<br><br>
<br><br>
Kero One &amp; Azure 兩位韓裔美籍嘻哈界指標人物的 2017暑期全新合作專輯，結合了爵士嘻哈新曲風與充滿故事性的饒舌內容，創造了獨一無二的都會嘻哈聲音。頂級音樂製作人 Kero One 與來自美國知名饒舌團體 Hbk Gang 的 Azure 這次合作的音樂呈現更是突破了主流嘻哈框架，希望找出嘻哈最單純的感染力。
</div>
