## 简介

<div>
For my latest track, and third remix of the summer season, I remixed Nico &amp; Vinz's brilliant "Am I Wrong." On this one I tried to bring my own touch to the track, while adding some new percussive and orchestral flavors to complement the guitar melodies. I hope this track helps you wind down your summer's appropriately!
