## 简介

<div>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin-bottom: 24px; border: 0px; font-family: Lato, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; outline: 0px; vertical-align: ba<x>seline; text-align: justify; color: rgb(64, 64, 64);">Sheet music available: <a href="https://www.isaacshepard.com/sheet-music/" title="" target="_blank"></a><a href="https://www.isaacshepard.com/sheet-music/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener">https://www.isaacshepard.com/sheet-music/</a></p><br>

Follow these imaginative melodies as they set the backdrop for the classic story of a beautiful lady who is imprisoned by a powerful dragon and then rescued by a knight in shining armor. The relationship does not turn out as expected at first, but through perseverance and faith they find true love.</p><br>
