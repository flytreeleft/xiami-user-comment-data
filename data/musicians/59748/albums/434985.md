## 简介

<div>
Stir your emotions with Awaken, Isaac Shepard's fourth solo piano CD. These original neo-classical compositions will inspire you with their simplicity and enliven you with their depth. <br><br>
<br><br>
Isaac's latest offering stands solidly on the pillars of his three previous solo piano albums (Swept Away, Deep Joy, and The Renewing), gracefully blending beautiful ballads with unforgettable melodies.
