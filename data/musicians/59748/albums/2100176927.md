## 简介

<div>
<p> Sheet music available: <a href="https://www.isaacshepard.com/sheet-music/" title="" target="_blank"></a><a href="https://www.isaacshepard.com/sheet-music/" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noreferrer noopener">https://www.isaacshepard.com/sheet-music/</a></p><br>
<p><a href="https://www.isaacshepard.com" title="" target="_blank" style="font-family: Lato, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: justify;"></a></p><br>
<p><a href="https://www.isaacshepard.com" title="" target="_blank" style="font-family: Lato, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: justify;"></a><span style="color: rgb(64, 64, 64); font-family: Lato, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; text-align: justify;">Isaac Shepard’s sixth solo piano CD, Mosaic, contains twelve elegant, easy-listening contemporary piano tracks. These peaceful and intriguing modern solo piano songs are great for work, study, meditation, relaxation, and life.</span></p><br>
<p style="box-sizing: border-box; margin-bottom: 24px; border: 0px; font-family: Lato, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 16px; outline: 0px; vertical-align: baxseline; text-align: justify; color: rgb(64, 64, 64);">Included in Mosaic are re-recordings of four popular songs that were on his first two solo piano albums over six years ago. This includes re-recordings of Before Dawn from <a href="https://www.isaacshepard.com/music/swept-away" style="box-sizing: border-box; background: 0px 0px; color: rgb(30, 114, 189); text-decoration: none; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-weight: inherit; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baxseline; transition: all 700ms;">Swept Away,</a> as well as Letting Go, Gentle, and Leaves in the Wind from <a href="https://www.isaacshepard.com/music/deep-joy" style="box-sizing: border-box; background: 0px 0px; color: rgb(30, 114, 189); text-decoration: none; border: 0px; font-family: inherit; font-style: inherit; font-weight: inherit; margin: 0px; outline: 0px; padding: 0px; vertical-align: baxseline; transition: all 700ms;">Deep Joy.</a> These songs were specially re-recorded at a higher quality and with less dynamics so that they are more suitable for easy-listening situations. The result: these new recordings are more intimate versions of the originals that fit perfectly into the Mosaic theme.</p><br>
<p> </p>
