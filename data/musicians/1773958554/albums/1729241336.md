## 简介

<div>
The third instalment in our JET JAM Mixtape series is courtesy of our Parisian friend FKJ aka French Kiwi Juice. He caught our ears a few months ago with his brand of uber-sexy disco on So Much To Me, which is finally to be released on his latest EP for Roche Musique. We then booked him for the party we put on in Nottingham earlier this month and he completely smashed it. Playing many upfront exclusives alongside classics.<br><br>
