color: black; font-size: 9pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 宋体; mso-font-kerning: 0pt">直抒胸臆的歌词，痴情的为爱呼唤，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 9pt; mso-ascii-font-family: " new="" times="">旋律煽情，朗朗上口，极具流行性</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
