## 简介

<div>
Songwriting is a very personal process for Joshua Radin; his songs capture the listener and keep them entranced from beginning to end. Each moment can become a snapshot in time generated by a specific creative spark for this upcoming artist -- whose hit song "Winter" was recently featured on the NBC television sit-com Scrubs. <br><br>
Joshua began his artistic pursuits at an early age with art, acting and music. He started on his musical path with songwriters like Lennon and McCartney, and is currently likened to writers like Elliot Smith and Bob Dylan. His worldwide exposure garnered interest from thousands of fans who were eagerly downloading "Winter" and seeking to purchase his upcoming CD entitled: "First Between 3rd &amp; 4th". <br><br>
<br><br>
Product Description<br><br>
"First Between 3rd &amp; 4th" is a glimpse of what is to come from Joshua Radin. A singer/songwriter whose writing is both musically and lyricly personal and engaging -- his album is a captivating mix of alternative rock and folk. <br><br>
