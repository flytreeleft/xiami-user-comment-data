## 简介

<div>
<p>With the time moving forward, with the wind passing by, with the river of life going on, note every moment touched here, in this "LISTEN". This "LISTEN" will be an updating music note for my life.<br><br>
<br><br>
时间一直往前，风吹过耳边，生命之河不息，在这个《Listen》中，记录每一个触动的瞬间。因此这会是一张不断在更新的生活音乐笔记。</p>
</div>
