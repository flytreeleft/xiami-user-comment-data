## 简介

<div>
I wanted this Album to have a big range in terms of emotions and moods. So I picked old ones and recent ones to record, as I've written many songs after I made "There".<br><br>
It turned out that this Album is the best thing to describe what I am right now.<br><br>
These years made such a big difference in my life. I always move and change place to live. I've never settled down in one place for about two years. I really thank you for everyone who supports me. I couldn't have finished this done without your supports. I do hope that you will enjoy and love this new Album.<br><br>
<br><br>
by Yuto Uchino
</div>
