## 简介

<div>
<p>既2020年11月发行了单曲《Sapphire》之后，The fin.决定在即将于2021年中发行的新专辑之前先发行第2首单曲《Deepest Ocean》（两首都收录在新专辑中）。新曲是由Yuto Uchino创作、演奏并制作。由洛杉矶的格莱美奖获奖音乐工程师Mike Bozzi来进行母带的后期调音，The fin.优雅的音乐达到了新的质量水平。</p><p></p><p>After &amp;Sapphire&amp; in November, The fin. starts 2021 by dropping the 2nd single ahead of their upcoming album, set for release in 2021. Yuto Uchino composed, played and self-produced &amp;Deepest Ocean&amp;, adding a refined funky hook to the creative blend of dream-pop and psychedelic synth-pop that is The fin.’s soundscape. Mastered by Mike Bozzi, Grammy award winning engineer from LA, The fin.'s elegant soundscape has reached a new level of quality.</p><p></p><p>Comment from Yuto Uchino (The fin.) :</p><p></p><p>&amp; I wrote Deepest Ocean in 2018 when I kept moving since we were having so many shows all over the world at the moment. It was entertaining, but I was feeling unsettled at the same time. I found that I was searching for an ideal world that exists only in my mind when I wrote it. It’s about escapism. But it has the strong energy to fight with reality and my self at the moment.&amp;</p>
</div>
