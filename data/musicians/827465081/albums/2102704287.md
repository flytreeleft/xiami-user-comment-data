<p>  </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> WHEN YOU ARE OLD 当你老了 </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> 【William Butler Yeats 威廉·巴特勒·叶芝】 </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> When you are old and grey and full of sleep, 当你老了，头发花白，睡意沉沉， </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> And nodding by the fire，take down this book, 倦坐在炉边，取下这本书来， </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> And slowly read,and dream of the soft look 慢慢读着，追梦当年的眼神 </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> Your eyes had once,and of their shadows deep; 你那柔美的神采与深幽的晕影。 </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> How many loved your moments of glad grace, 多少人爱过你昙花一现的身影， </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> And loved your beauty with love false or true, 爱过你的美貌，以虚伪或真情， </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> But one man loved the pilgrim Soul in you 惟独一人曾爱你那朝圣者的心， </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> And loved the sorrows of your changing face; 爱你哀戚的脸上岁月的留痕。 </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> And bending down beside the glowing bars, 在炉罩边低眉弯腰， </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> Murmur,a little sadly,how Love fled 忧戚沉思，喃喃而语， </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> And paced upon the mountains overhead 爱情是怎样逝去，又怎样步上群山， </p><br>
<p class="&amp;f-brk&quot;"> And hid his face amid a crowd of stars. 怎样在繁星之间藏住了脸。 </p><br>
