## 简介

<div>
<p>The Shanghai Restoration Project comes back once again with a lovely remix album of old chinese music with new beats and instrumentals in Remixed and Restored. The songs retain their cultured antiquity while featuring the traditional/modern mix of beat that we've come to expect from the Shanghai Restoration Project. This one's probably my favorite of the releases so far because of the classic chinese songs at the core.  </p>
