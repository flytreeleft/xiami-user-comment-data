## 简介

<div>
<p>Bridging the cultural gap between East and West, the artist/producer known as the SHANGHAI RESTORATION PROJECT has put together the ultimate collection of music for your ultimate 24 Hours in his namesake city. Features select instrumentals from the albums THE SHANGHAI RESTORATION PROJECT: SPECIAL EDITION and STORY OF A CITY along with several previously unreleased tracks.</p><br>
