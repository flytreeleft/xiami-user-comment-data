## 简介

<div>
About&nbsp;Free2sound<br><br>
Meditative&nbsp;improvisation,&nbsp;experimental&nbsp;electro-acoustic&nbsp;music.<br><br>
Mixing&nbsp;the&nbsp;"ideas"&nbsp;of&nbsp;western&nbsp;and&nbsp;eastern&nbsp;music.&nbsp;Through&nbsp;qin,&nbsp;vocals,&nbsp;loopstation,&nbsp;electronic&nbsp;pedals&nbsp;we&nbsp;create&nbsp;unique&nbsp;music.&nbsp;It&nbsp;is&nbsp;directly&nbsp;possible&nbsp;to&nbsp;create&nbsp;a&nbsp;realistic&nbsp;mix&nbsp;between&nbsp;western&nbsp;and&nbsp;eastern&nbsp;culture&nbsp;through&nbsp;playing&nbsp;improvised&nbsp;music&nbsp;together.&nbsp;We&nbsp;already&nbsp;from&nbsp;the&nbsp;start&nbsp;have&nbsp;the&nbsp;will&nbsp;to&nbsp;create&nbsp;freely.&nbsp;Our&nbsp;sounds&nbsp;are&nbsp;interwoven&nbsp;naturally&nbsp;and&nbsp;becomes&nbsp;exactly&nbsp;what&nbsp;we&nbsp;want&nbsp;to&nbsp;express&nbsp;and&nbsp;share&nbsp;with&nbsp;others&nbsp;-&nbsp;That&nbsp;it's&nbsp;easy&nbsp;to&nbsp;share&nbsp;ideas&nbsp;when&nbsp;you&nbsp;have&nbsp;the&nbsp;curiosity&nbsp;to&nbsp;create&nbsp;a&nbsp;"new&nbsp;space".&nbsp;<br><br>
Zeng Xiaogang&nbsp;is&nbsp;qin&nbsp;player, and&nbsp;free&nbsp;musician&nbsp;from&nbsp;Yunnan,&nbsp;China.&nbsp;Xiaogang&nbsp;moved&nbsp;to&nbsp;Sweden&nbsp;in&nbsp;2007&nbsp;and&nbsp;2009-2012&nbsp;he&nbsp;studied&nbsp;at&nbsp;the&nbsp;World&nbsp;Music&nbsp;Program&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;Gothenburg's&nbsp;Academy&nbsp;for&nbsp;Drama&nbsp;and&nbsp;Music.&nbsp;Experiences&nbsp;from&nbsp;both&nbsp;western&nbsp;and&nbsp;eastern&nbsp;music&nbsp;give&nbsp;him&nbsp;great&nbsp;inspiration&nbsp;for&nbsp;creating&nbsp;new&nbsp;qin&nbsp;music.<br><br>
<br><br>
Hannah&nbsp;Tolf&nbsp;is&nbsp;a&nbsp;singer&nbsp;and&nbsp;composer&nbsp;from&nbsp;Gothenburg,&nbsp;Sweden.&nbsp;She&nbsp;has&nbsp;studied&nbsp;improvisation&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;Skurups&nbsp;community&nbsp;college&nbsp;and&nbsp;the&nbsp;Gothenburg's&nbsp;Academy&nbsp;for&nbsp;Drama&nbsp;and&nbsp;Music.&nbsp;She&nbsp;has&nbsp;in&nbsp;the&nbsp;constellation&nbsp;Hannah&nbsp;Tolf&nbsp;and&nbsp;Dick&nbsp;Ahlin&nbsp;played&nbsp;at&nbsp;many&nbsp;Blues.
</div>
