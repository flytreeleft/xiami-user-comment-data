## 简介

<div>
<p> </p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">《十三・秋》</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">尺八：李嘉徵</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">古琴<span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: " helvetica="" neue";"="">/</span>編曲：曾小剛</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">縮混：韋敬民</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">插畫：林妲</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " helvetica="" neue";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);="" min-height:="" 14px;"=""> </p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">秋聲又入梧桐。</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">那年作的曲子，今得嘉徵尺八加入，重新編配之後，愈給人蒼涼遼窅之感。猶如時令向更深遠的地方延伸，秋意一陣緊似一陣。<span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: " helvetica="" neue";"=""> </span></p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">那年隨興在枯桐上的抹挑勾剔,<span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: " helvetica="" neue";"="">  </span>響在十三徽之間，盡十三徽之聲而巧合為十三拍。</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">那年立秋也在今天。十指生秋水，炎夏薄衣雖未脫去，七弦上，已然秋聲清越。</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">《樂記》：樂者，音之所由生也，其本在心之感於物也。感於物而動，故形於聲。</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">萬物之生滅，何嘗不是應時而動。</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">後才知道，原來寫它的那年，正好有十三個月。</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">後又聽說，每逢十三個月的那年，梧桐枝上會生十三葉。</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">而今又得知，立秋乃二十四節氣中之第十三<span style="font-stretch: normal; line-height: normal; font-family: " helvetica="" neue";"="">......</span></p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">或者純屬巧合。</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">或者梧桐有知，所以用「知閏、知秋」來詮釋一平年十二葉閏年則十三葉。多出來的那一葉，那一拍，許是詮釋另一種「得一」之妙吧。所謂一即自然。</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">古人言自然之數為十三。而陽律陰呂，清濁高下，其聲之所發又皆應自然之節,，合於天地之數。</p><br>
<p style="font-stretch: normal; font-size: 12px; line-height: normal; font-family: " .pingfang="" sc";="" color:="" rgb(69,="" 69,="" 69);"="">當年這支曲子，不為而成，可算是得於陰陽，而合於術數。今以「十三・秋」名之，亦是應時對景，歸復自然。</p><br>
