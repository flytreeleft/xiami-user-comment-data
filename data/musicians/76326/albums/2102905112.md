## 简介

<div>
安静的夜晚，适合与最真实的自己独处。2017年初，因《我是歌手》节目带来巨大关注度的赵雷却毅然拒绝了节目后过度的曝光、演出以及商业合作的机会。他安安静静将自己清零，把大部分时间专注于创作，让自己回归起点。今天，赵雷只用这首新歌诉出心声：“我想应该静下来想一些话，走一段路，看一本书。”上帝给予每个人的时光都有限，我们该做些什么？唯有让自己静下来，才会听到灵魂之所向。<br><br>
<br><br>
In silent nights, the best thing to do is spending alone with the truly self.<br><br>
<br><br>
After the appearance on the famous TV show—I Am a Singer , Zhao Lei has received huge interests from the public from the beginning of 2017. However, he chose to clear himself rather than to be over exposed by performances and advertisements<br><br>
<br><br>
In these days, Zhao Lei spent most of his time to concentrate on creating songs, and get back to the basics.<br><br>
<br><br>
Today, Zhao Lei opens his heart in the new song: “I should slow down the footsteps, to read a book, take a walk, and think about some truth.” The time we received from God is limited, so what should we do? Slow down, and we can finally hear the wants from our spirits.<br>
</div>
