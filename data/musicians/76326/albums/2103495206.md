## 简介

<div>
年少的速写时光，心底的镌刻记忆。逆着时光回望，对过去的自己说一声：“我记得你。”怀旧如赵雷，他用音乐穿越回自己的19岁：初识的游泳馆，康铭旋转门，宽街路口，红绿灯下的驻足……赵雷把发生在那个年代纯美的青春故事，唱给你听。<br><br>
<br><br>
Recollecting those memories of the time that spending on sketching, Zhao Lei looks back into his youth and tells the young guy “I remember you.” For cherishing the past, music remains Zhao Lei in the age of 19: the swimming pool that we first met, Kang Ming revolution door, Broad Street, the halt under traffic lights…Zhao Lei will sing it to you, the pure and beautiful story of the youth in 19.
</div>
