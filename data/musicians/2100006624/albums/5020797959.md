## 简介

<div>
<p>这是一首热血的歌，送给最厉害的老爸。</p><p>家里买了新车，承载着许多回忆的“破车”就要退休了。最后一次出发，它轰鸣的引擎声如同临别的碎碎念，寄托着对这个家庭满满的不舍。</p><p>————————————</p><p>“我见过无数的风景 我路过黑夜和黎明”</p><p>“我遇到过老爸一人在深夜 给自己鼓励”</p><p>“这个家 就交给你来保护咯”</p><p>“跑起来吧！”</p><p></p><p>词/曲：金承志</p><p>指挥：金承志</p><p>演唱：上海彩虹室内合唱团</p><p>钢琴伴奏：吴经纬</p><p>录音/混音：莫家伟</p><p>录制地点：上海东方艺术中心</p><p></p><p>It’s a motivating song, dedicated to all fantastic dads around the world.</p><p>We got a new car in the family, so that its predecessor loaded with familial recollections would be retiring soon. Now it’s the last time the old car was in service, and its whirring engine seemed to be expressing its reluctance to retire.</p><p>————————————</p><p>“I have witnessed the natural beauty. And driven through numerous nights and mornings.”</p><p>“I have once seen Dad being in the car at night. Encouraging himself all alone.”</p><p>“Please serve the family in place of me. And be the guardian of them.”</p><p>“Hit the road!”</p><p></p><p>Music/ lyrics: Chengzhi Jin</p><p>Conductor: Chengzhi Jin</p><p>Performer: Shanghai Rainbow Chamber Singers</p><p>Piano accompanist: Jingwei Wu</p><p>Recording and Mixing Engineer: Jiawei Mo </p><p>Recording venue: Shanghai Oriental Art Center</p>
