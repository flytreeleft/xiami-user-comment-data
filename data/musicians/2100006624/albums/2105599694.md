## 简介

<div>
“从前的日色变得慢<br><br>
车 马 邮件都慢<br><br>
一生只够爱一个人”<br><br>
<br><br>
木心先生的《从前慢》用简短的语句把人们带回了那些被怀念追忆的过去，<br><br>
现在上海彩虹室内合唱团重新演绎了刘胡轶作曲的改编作品，希望让旋律带着词句进入听者的心里，拾起那些温情的旧时光。<br><br>
<br><br>
词：木心<br><br>
曲：刘胡轶<br><br>
合唱改编：金承志<br><br>
演唱：上海彩虹室内合唱团<br><br>
指挥：金承志<br><br>
钢琴伴奏：白苑彤<br><br>
录音：莫家伟<br><br>
录制地点：上海东方艺术中心<br><br>
<br><br>
“Days were slower in the past, <br><br>
When carriages and mails didn’t run fast,<br><br>
When it’d take a life-time to be wedded to the one you loved.”<br><br>
<br><br>
Penned by the renowned Chinese poet Muxin, the poem “Time Went Slower in the Past”, with its concise verses, had left generations of readers reminiscing about their past.<br><br>
Now the Shanghai Rainbow Chamber Singers are rendering this poem in a chorus composed by Huyi Liu, with the intention of moving the audience with its mollifying melody and beautiful verses to the extent that they will recall their respective heart-warming past moments.<br><br>
<br><br>
Music by Huyi Liu<br><br>
Lyrics by Muxin<br><br>
Performer: Shanghai Rainbow Chamber Singers<br><br>
Piano accompanist: Yuantong Bai<br><br>
Recording and Mixing Engineer: Jiawei Mo<br><br>
Recording Place: Shanghai Oriental Art Center
</div>
