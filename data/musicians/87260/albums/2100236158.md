## 简介

<div>
<p><span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 153);">IIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIII虾米音乐·全网独家IIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIIII</span><br><br>
<br><br>
Focusing even further into his soundtrack vibe, Senpai created this album with a deep feeling toward being stuck in time. Everyone has felt stuck in place while watching the world continue to move, and that exact feeling is depicted throughout this album. You may describe the sound of the album to be on the sad end, but each song is meant to let the listener know that they are not alone with this feeling. “When Time is Still” will ease your mind and allow you to rest on those long, dark nights.</p>
