## 简介

<div>
LYFE Team wish you a warm Christmas!<br><br>
Hello friends,<br><br>
<br><br>
Here's what I promised you.  Christmas songs at last! They are free, free as in free beer and also, religion free. Christmas in Asia has its own tradition and own life, but I think universally we all could find our own happiness in it. I had fun making these songs, I hope you will enjoy them: Simple Wish, Santa Claus is Coming to Town, First Noel, and Away in a Manger.<br><br>
<br><br>
Here I, on behalf of the whole LYFE Team, wish you a warm Christmas.
</div>
